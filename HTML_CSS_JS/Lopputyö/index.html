Why is design thinking
important?
Design thinking enables organizations to create lasting value of consumers.
The process is useful in any complex systems (not just design systems) because it:

  • Aims to solve a concrete human need
  • Tackles problems that are ambiguous or difficult to define
  • Leads to more innovative solutions
  • Makes organizations run faster and more efficiently

Source: wework.com
